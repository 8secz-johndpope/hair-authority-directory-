ISHRS

The International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS) – Look for this symbol if you are considering hair transplant surgery. ISHRS is a global non-profit medical association and a leading authority on hair loss treatment and restoration. With more than 1,100 members throughout 70 countries, ISHRS is dedicated to achieving excellence in patient outcomes by promoting the highest standards of medical practice, medical ethics, and research in the medical hair restoration industry.

ARTAS Robotic Hair Restoration System – Leading hair restoration physicians and researchers developed the ARTAS Robotic Hair Restoration System to eliminate the fatigue, linear scarring and human error sometimes associated with other procedures. WTS

The World Trichology Society – WTS is an organization that promotes the science of trichology. Trichology is the para-medical science of the hair , hair loss and associated scalp problems. It is the “bridge between cosmetology and dermatology.” Certified WTS Trichologists have successfully completed a rigorous course covering medical and cosmetic hair loss solutions.
